Aglio Olio Recipe

Description

An easy and simple pasta to make with ingredients you have in your home!

Ingredients

  • Angel hair pasta
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Chilli
  • Salt and sugar to taste
  • Parmesan cheese and parsley

Steps

  1. Boil water, olive oil and salt. Add pasta and cook till aldante.
  2. Chop garlic, chilli and parsley
  3. Drain and put pasta aside
  4. Heat up pan, add olive oil and fry garlic till golden brown.
  5. Add pasta in pan, then salt and sugar to taste
  6. Add parsley and simmer till ready to serve
  7. Serve with parmesan cheese and enjoy!
