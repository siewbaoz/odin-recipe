Pasta Recipe

Description

This is a simple and easy to make tomato pasta recipe. Have this for a meal in less than an hour!

Ingredients

  • Barilla No.4 Spaghetti
  • Olive Oil
  • Garlic
  • Button mushrooms
  • Salt to taste
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Barilla Tomato Sauce

Steps

  1. Cook Barilla No.4 Spaghetti till aldante
  2. Drain and keep 1 cup of pasta water
  3. Heat olive oil, fry garlic and sliced button mushrooms
  4. Once garlic is golden brown, add Barilla Tomato Sauce
  5. Add pasta water and let boil for 3 minutes
  6. Salt to taste and add Barilla No.4 Spaghetti into sauce
  7. Serve with Parmesan Cheese
